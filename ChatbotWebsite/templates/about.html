About Us
Welcome to our mental health chatbot. Our mission is to provide reliable information and resources to help you improve your mental health and wellbeing in a safe, private, and supportive environment.
We understand that seeking help for mental health concerns can be challenging. Our chatbot is designed to make the process as comfortable and accessible as possible, offering guidance and support at your own pace.
Note: Our chatbot is not a substitute for professional help. If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, please seek assistance from a qualified mental health professional.
Our Mission
We believe that education and awareness are critical components of mental health. Our chatbot provides accurate and up-to-date information on various mental health conditions, including symptoms, causes, and effects. We also offer resources such as mental health tests and exercises to help you evaluate and improve your wellbeing.
✓ Evidence-based information
✓ Anonymous and private support
✓ Accessible anytime, anywhere
